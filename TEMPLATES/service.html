Surgical Procedures

Our surgical department offers a wide range of procedures performed by experienced surgeons using the latest techniques and equipment. We prioritize patient safety and comfort throughout the surgical process.

Our general surgery services include procedures such as appendectomy, hernia repair, gallbladder removal, and other common surgical interventions. Our surgeons use minimally invasive techniques when possible to reduce recovery time.

Our orthopedic surgeons specialize in procedures such as joint replacements, arthroscopy, fracture repair, and spinal surgery. We use advanced techniques and materials to ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients.

We offer a variety of minimally invasive surgical options, including laparoscopic and endoscopic procedures. These techniques typically result in smaller incisions, less pain, and faster recovery times compared to traditional open surgery.

Emergency Care

Our emergency department is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing immediate care for acute illnesses and injuries. Our team of emergency medicine specialists is equipped to handle a wide range of medical emergencies.

When to Visit the Emergency Department:
  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Severe abdominal pain
  • Sudden severe headache
  • Major injuries or trauma
  • Uncontrolled bleeding
