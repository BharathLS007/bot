Our Mission & Vision

Guided by our core values, we strive to make a positive impact on the health and wellbeing of our community.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional healthcare services that improve the health and wellbeing of the individuals and communities we serve. We are committed to delivering compassionate, high-quality care that puts patients first.

We strive to make healthcare accessible to all and to continuously improve our services through innovation, education, and research.

Our Vision

To be the healthcare provider of choice, recognized for excellence in patient care, innovative medical practices, and contributions to the advancement of medicine.

We envision a future where everyone has access to quality healthcare, where preventive care is emphasized, and where technology and human compassion work together to create the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our Journey

From our humble beginnings to where we are today, we've been committed to improving healthcare for over two decades.

2003

Foundation

MediCare was founded with a vision to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to the community. Starting with just 5 doctors and a small clinic, we began our journey of healing and care.

2008

Expansion

We expanded our facilities to include specialized departments for cardiology, pediatrics, and orthopedics. Our team grew to 20 doctors and 50 support staff, allowing us to serve more patients.

2015

Technology Integration

We invested in cutting-edge medical technology and digital health solutions, including electronic health records and telemedicine capabilities, enhancing our ability to provide efficient and effective care.

2023

Today

Today, MediCare is a comprehensive healthcare center with over 50 doctors, 150 staff members, and state-of-the-art facilities. We continue to grow and evolve, always focused on our mission to provide exceptional care to our community.
